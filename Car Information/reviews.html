Reviews of Honda Civic Sports Sedan

U.S. News Expert Review

Zach Doell is a vehicle testing editor for U.S. News Best Cars. He strives to demystify the car buying process and believes that there's a perfect vehicle out there for everyone.

Below is his review on Honda Civic Sports Sedan.

The Honda Civic has traditionally been a good buy if you want an affordable, fuel-efficient and dependable compact car. This latest generation, however, elevates the Civic from safe bet to a truly desirable choice. I rented a Civic Sport sedan (MSRP: $25,550) on Turo for this evaluation, and I found it to be a pleasure to drive, with spry handling, an upscale cabin and admirable fuel economy. It's a family-friendly sedan that doesn't feel like a cheap conveyance, and apart from the similarly posh Mazda3, I think the Civic stands a cut above rivals like the Toyota Corolla Nissan Sentra and Hyundai Elantra .
