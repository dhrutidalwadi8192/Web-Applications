Honda Civic Sedan Sports 2024

Overview

The Honda Civic Sedan has been one of my favourite cars since my childhood. In India, when I see that car around me, I always think to buy it when I start earning. I am glad to know that it was Canada's best-selling car for 24 years. But in 2022, this car lost its title for best seller. In the same year, Honda launched a sports model of its Civic sedan. There is not any notable change in the 2024 model.

The new Honda Civic Sport trim is one step up from the LX trim, with all the standard features of a Civic sedan and additional athletic features. The sport trim has aluminium sport pedals that feel amazing on your feet and a 6-speed manual transmission, so drivers can change gear manually.

Highlights

  • Standard 6-speed manual transmission and limited-slip differential
  • 200-horsepower 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-four cylinder engine
  • Better Audio System - 12 Speaker Bose® premium sound system
  • Amazing safety features

Pricing for this car starts at $32,714.

The car may not be the best car of its category , but it is definitely one of the most fuel efficient. It manages an impressive 37 MPG in mixed driving conditions which is far better than its competitors. Golf GTI (27 MPG) or Hyundai Elantra N (24 MPG).

The manufacturer Honda has used its own sensing technologies and additional safety features.

  • sign recognition displays traffic sign information to assist driver while driving and the camera is designed to recognize traffic signs.
  • Lane Keeping Assistant System (LKAS) gently correct your steering if you begin to leave a detected lane without signaling.
  • Forward Collision Warning (FCW) is designed to detect the presence of vehicles in front of you and issue a audible,visual and tactlie alerts if you are approaching with too much speed.
  • The Collision Mitigation Braking System™ (CMBS®) is engineered to apply light brake pressure if you don't respond to FCW system alerts.

Exterior colours

This civic sport is available with five color options.

  • Crystal Black Pearl
  • Platinum White Pearl
  • Sonic Grey Pearl
  • Ralley Red
  • Aegean Blue Metallic

The choice of color is subjective but Rallye Red and Crystal Black Pearl are popular for this sport trim.

Interior Features

If we talk about the interior, the car is sporty inside. It has red accents, which complement the black and red sport seats perfectly. Red contrast stitching on the steering wheel, doors, and shifter makes us feel like we are driving a carefully designed sports car. The car is very special and heated in all the right places. The car ride is comfortable for rear passengers as well. High quality materials are used throughout the cabin.

Technology

  • 9" colour touchscreen is centralized system for setting cntrols and technology features
  • Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ compatibility
  • Blind Spot Information (BSI) System & Rear Cross Traffic Monitor System
    • BSI system is designed to detect and alert you of any vehicles in your blind spot when signalling a lane change.
  • Wireless Charging
  • Remote Engine Start & Proximity Key Entry System
    • The proximity key entry system unlocks your door automatically, so all you have to do is get in and go.
  • USB port
  • Push-button start

Summary

To summarize, this car is good option to buy when you are looking for a car fo daily use. The car is enjoyable to drive, cmfartable for rear passanger as well, spacious and has a good fuel economy. The car has received commendable 5-star overall safety rating in NHTSA Crash Testing and there have been no recalls or consumer complaints for the current generation, suggesting that it will be a reliable vehicle to own in the long run.
